The Fractal approach

Fractal bookkeeping treats the books like a living map: every detail is accurate on its own, and also clearly connects to the pattern of the whole business.

Zoom in: transactions are categorized consistently, with clean supporting detail.

Zoom out: statements roll up into a narrative that matches how decisions are made.

Repeat: a monthly rhythm keeps everything aligned as the business changes.

A simple monthly cadence

  1. Collect: bank, card, bills, and invoices are captured consistently.
  2. Close: reconcile, categorize, and resolve exceptions.
  3. Explain: deliver statements + a short narrative on what changed.
  4. Improve: refine rules so next month closes faster and cleaner.
